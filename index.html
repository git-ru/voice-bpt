In rehearsing the unknown together,
Soundtrack for My Funeral
treats

the unseen as inspiration.


Here, audio description — usually a tool for making visual media accessible to blind and low-vision audiences — becomes a way of

making meaning together.

what do you see?
what do you notice?


How would you describe this to someone in another room?
It can be observational, poetic, personal — or all three.
However you choose to do this, your perspective is welcome.


Multiple voices describe the same work, every perspective its own. What emerges is collective —

living.

to participate, scan the QR code beside any artwork in the lēʻahi gallery. download the map to find each curator's code.
